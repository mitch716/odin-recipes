Penne Alla Vodka

Description

Penne alla Vodka features al dente penne pasta coated in a velvety sauce made from a blend of tomatoes, heavy cream, and a splash of vodka. The vodka, while not giving the sauce a pronounced alcoholic taste, helps to meld the flavors together and adds a subtle complexity. The sauce is usually enriched with garlic, onions, and a touch of red pepper flakes for a hint of heat. The dish is often finished with freshly grated Parmesan cheese and a sprinkle of fresh basil or parsley.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Vodka Sauce
  • Salt, Sugar, Pepper

Steps

  1. Cook the Pasta
  2. Prepare the Sauce
  3. Combine Pasta and Sauce
  4. Garnish and Serve
